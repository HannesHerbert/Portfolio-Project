Hannes Petri - Fullstack Web Developer & Designer

Originally trained as a laboratory technician I started a lateral entry in web developement in 2022 and immediately got hooked by the possibilities this work has to offer. It turned right into a passion to create individual webspaces for clients with different demands, whether they seek functional and/or design-requirements.

I love to work together with different people since you can profit from each other in the way they think, plan and progress in their projects. This characteristic grew as I assisted in several research-projects in my previous career at the University in Leipzig where quality management and communication were crucial to obtain qualitative and satisfying results.

  • SASS/SCSS
  • Javascript
  • Bootstrap/Wordpress
  • MongoDB
